Combines 2011 census data from:
http://www.nomisweb.co.uk/census/2011/postcode_headcounts_and_household_estimates
with Ordnance Survey OpenData codepoint data from:
https://www.ordnancesurvey.co.uk/opendatadownload/products.html

Processing steps:
1) Download data (October 2016)
2) Some preprocessing steps
3) Join headcounts to Postcodes
4) Clip to Wales Principal Areas
5) Output to Shapefile in OSGB coordinates

Adrian Luckman, october 2016
